These mods enhance your Minecraft experience with useful features that don't change core gameplay. From maps to inventory management, these additions make playing more enjoyable.

## Navigation

### Xaero's Minimap
**Always know where you are.**

Adds a minimap to your HUD showing nearby terrain, entities, and waypoints.

**Key Features:**
- Customizable minimap position and size
- Entity radar (players, mobs, items)
- Waypoint system
- Death point markers
- Cave mode

**Default Key:** Press **M** to open settings

### Xaero's World Map
**Full-screen world map.**

A complete world map that fills in as you explore, with teleportation support (in creative/with cheats).

**Key Features:**
- Auto-mapping as you explore
- Shared waypoints with minimap
- Coordinate display
- Biome overlay

**Default Key:** Press **M** (hold) or configure separately

### Nature's Compass
**Find any biome.**

A craftable compass that points you toward any biome you're searching for.

**Crafting:** Surround a regular compass with saplings

**Usage:** Right-click to open biome selection

## Information & Tooltips

### Jade
**Know what you're looking at.**

Shows information about blocks and entities when you look at them. Health, inventory contents, growth stages, and more.

**Features:**
- Block information
- Entity health bars
- Container previews
- Crop growth stages
- Mod-specific info

**Settings:** Middle-click on any block to configure

<Aside type="tip">
Jade can be configured per-block-type. Don't want to see certain info? Disable it!
</Aside>

### AppleSkin
**Understand your hunger.**

Shows how much hunger and saturation food will restore before you eat it.

**Features:**
- Food value preview
- Saturation overlay
- Exhaustion visualization
- Health regeneration preview

### Better Mount HUD
**Improved mount interface.**

When riding horses, shows the mount's health, jump bar, and your hunger simultaneously.

## Inventory Management

### Inventory Profiles Next
**Smart inventory sorting and management.**

Powerful inventory management with sorting, auto-refill, and profiles.

**Key Features:**
- One-click inventory sorting
- Locked slots
- Auto-refill hotbar items
- Inventory profiles
- Move matching items

**Default Keys:**
| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| **R** | Sort inventory |
| **Scroll** in inventory | Move items |
| **Middle-click** | Lock slot |

### Controlify
**Full controller support.**

Play Minecraft with a controller! Full button mapping and vibration support.

**Features:**
- Auto-detect controllers
- Customizable mappings
- Vibration feedback
- On-screen button prompts

**Settings:** Mods → Controlify

## Visual Improvements

### Mod Menu
**Configure all your mods.**

Provides an in-game menu to access every mod's settings.

**Access:** Escape → Mods

### Sodium Extra
**More Sodium options.**

Adds additional toggle options for Sodium like:
- Animation toggles
- Particle settings
- Detail options
- Fog controls

### Reese's Sodium Options
**Better options menu.**

Reorganizes Sodium's video settings into a cleaner, more intuitive layout.

### Zoomify
**Zoom in on distant things.**

A smooth, customizable zoom feature.

**Default Key:** **C** (hold to zoom)

**Settings:**
- Zoom level
- Zoom speed
- Cinematic camera while zooming

### Cubes Without Borders
**True borderless windowed.**

Removes the window border in windowed mode for a cleaner look.

### Smooth Scroll
**Smooth list scrolling.**

Makes scrolling in menus smooth instead of jumping item by item.

### Smooth GUI
**Animated menus.**

Adds smooth animations when opening and closing menus.

### Paginated Advancements
**Better advancement screen.**

Reorganizes the advancement screen to be more readable with pagination.

## Debugging & Info

### Debugify
**Fix vanilla bugs.**

Fixes numerous vanilla Minecraft bugs that Mojang hasn't addressed.

**Examples of fixes:**
- MC-26757: Vision is impaired in water
- MC-122477: Wrong position of lead attachment point
- MC-145929: Beds too dark

<Aside>
Debugify only fixes bugs - it doesn't change intended game behavior.
</Aside>

## Recommended Keybinds

Here's a summary of the most useful keys:

| Key | Action | Mod |
|-----|--------|-----|
| **R** | Sort inventory | Inventory Profiles Next |
| **C** | Zoom | Zoomify |
| **M** | Open map | Xaero's World Map |
| **Middle-click** | Lock slot / Jade settings | Various |
| **F3** | Debug screen | Vanilla |
